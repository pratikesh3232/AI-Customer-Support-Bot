RETURN & REFUND POLICY — TechNova Store
========================================
Last updated: January 2025

1. RETURN ELIGIBILITY
----------------------
You may return most items within 30 days of the delivery date. To be eligible for a return:
- The item must be unused and in its original condition.
- All original packaging, accessories, manuals, and tags must be included.
- The return request must be initiated within 30 days of delivery.

Non-returnable items:
- Software products with broken seals or activated license keys.
- Consumable items such as batteries, ink cartridges, and printer paper once opened.
- Personalised or custom-engraved products.
- Items marked "Final Sale" at the time of purchase.

2. HOW TO INITIATE A RETURN
-----------------------------
Step 1: Log in to your TechNova account.
Step 2: Go to "My Orders" and select the order containing the item you wish to return.
Step 3: Click "Return Item" and select a reason from the dropdown.
Step 4: Choose your preferred return method: Pick-up (free) or Self-Ship.
Step 5: You will receive a confirmation email with return instructions within 24 hours.

For damaged or defective items, attach photos in the return form.

3. REFUND PROCESS
------------------
Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will notify you via email.

Refund timelines:
- Original payment method (card/net banking/UPI): 5–7 business days after approval.
- Store credit (wallet): 24–48 hours after approval.
- Cash on Delivery orders: Refunded as store credit or bank transfer within 7 business days.

Refunds will be for the full product price. Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (wrong item, damaged item).

4. EXCHANGE POLICY
-------------------
Exchanges are available for the same product in a different size or colour, subject to availability. To request an exchange, follow the same steps as a return and select "Exchange" instead of "Return".

5. DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
-------------------------------
If you receive a product that is damaged in transit or is defective:
- Contact us within 48 hours of delivery at support@technova.in.
- Include your order number, a description of the issue, and clear photographs.
- We will arrange a free pick-up and send a replacement within 3–5 business days, or issue a full refund if the item is out of stock.

6. WRONG ITEM RECEIVED
------------------------
If you received an item different from what you ordered:
- Contact support within 48 hours with your order number and a photo of the item received.
- We will arrange a free pick-up and deliver the correct item at no extra charge.

7. CANCELLATION POLICY
------------------------
Orders can be cancelled within 12 hours of placement for a full refund. After 12 hours, cancellation depends on shipment status. If already dispatched, please refuse delivery or initiate a return after receiving the item.
